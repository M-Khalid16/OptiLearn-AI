# OptiLearn AI

AI-Powered Educational Digital Twin for Optical Communication.

## Vision

OptiLearn AI transforms static optical communication lecture notes into interactive learning experiences.

Students can upload lecture notes and explore AI-generated explanations, visual simulations, and an educational digital twin to understand concepts such as:

- Optical modulation
- Signal propagation
- Chromatic dispersion
- Free Space Optical (FSO) communication
- Optical Fiber communication

Instead of simply reading equations, students can interact with system parameters and observe how they affect the transmitted and received optical signals.
